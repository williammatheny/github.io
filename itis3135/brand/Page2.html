Avalon Veterinary Hospital Clinic is located in In Broken Area part of Tulsa, Oklahoma (710 S 9th St, Broken Arrow, OK 74012). We provide service in the Broken Arrow area of Tulsa Oklahoma and the surrounding areas. We believe in not just helping animals when it comes to their health but also the bond they will have with you. We believe that creating a harmonious relationship between people and pets it will strengthen you both individually and together.

We always love getting new clients and love being able to help as many pets as we possibly can. Every time we get a new client we actually get two clients, you and your four-legged friend as we always treat you and the pets as if you are family. To get a hold of us please call (918) 258-9569. Thank you!
