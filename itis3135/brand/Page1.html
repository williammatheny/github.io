  • Dr Jennifer Morley - Has been in love with pets as long as she can remember. Becoming a veternarian was the only career-path she ever wanted. She never leaves an animal sick or in need without helping them. She always goes out of her way to help them and make sure they can love their best lives. Dr Jennier Morley lives on a 20-acre farm where they have several pets including four dogs, two cats, fish, a miniature pony, two quarter horses, and a gentle appaloosa.

    • Practice Manager

  • Holley - Holley has a deep love for animals as long as she can remember. She began working as a technician in Harrison, Arkansas and started training to become a veternarian shortly after. She helped with pets at clinics for the next six years. She then moved to Tulsa, Oklahoma and picked up the job at Avalon Clinic to help animals in need and has now been working here for the past four years.
