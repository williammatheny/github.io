Note from the author - I want to start this off by saying as the nephew of Dr Jennifer Morley her love for animals is one I haven't seen in anyone else. She is constantly doing what is best for her pets and others. I have heard several stories and phone calls of her pulling over on the side of the road if she sees an injured animal. She'll then take it in and heal it back to full health before releasing it back. It is truly amazing what she does and is extremely incredible the passion she has for all animals of all types.

Dr Jennifer Morley was born in the state of illinois. Where she lived with her mother Roberta and her father Thomas. She had a brother Michael, and a sister Sarah as well as a dog named Pepper. They were born and raised at this house from their birth to highschool and until they all went off to college.

She attended Illinois State University where she earned her bachelor's degree in biology and chemistry. After that she attended the University of Illinois College of Veterinary Medicine where she earned her Doctorate and became a licensed veternarian. Dr. Jennifer Morley went on to practice in Illinois, Indiana, and Oregon before she moved to Tulsa, Oklhahoma to continue for veterinary work. She joined the Avalon Veterinary hosptial in 2017.

Dr Morley and her husband Mark and have been together for 25 years and lived on a 20 acre farm with their two kids James and Jon. On this farm they have a variety of many animals. This includes dogs, cats, horses, ponies, fish, and chickens, there's always someone to keep you company there!
